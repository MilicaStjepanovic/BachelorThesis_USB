Popmap:
Reducta grouped as West, Italy, Balkans, East using Alena's new popmap.
Camilla labelled as cam.
Katia outgroups labelled as hel, pop, riv, sappho.

Main input folder:
~/reducta_clean/katia_outgroup/03_bams_all

Pipeline:
1. Mapped six Katia outgroups to L. reducta reference genome using bwa-mem2.
2. Combined 98 Reducta BAMs, 6 Camilla BAMs, and 6 Katia outgroup BAMs.
3. Checked that BAM basenames matched popmap sample names.
4. Ran gstacks on 110 BAMs.
5. Ran populations.
6. Filtered VCF with --max-missing 0.7 and no MAF filter.
7. Converted VCF to FASTA.
8. Ran IQ-TREE with -m MFP -bb 1000 -alrt 1000 on MetaCentrum PBS.
9. Visualised and rooted tree in FigTree on the Neptis outgroup clade.

Filtered alignment:
110 sequences
391,711 SNP columns

Final tree files:
~/reducta_clean/katia_outgroup/07_tree/FINAL_reducta_camilla_katia_ML_tree.treefile
~/reducta_clean/katia_outgroup/07_tree/FINAL_reducta_camilla_katia_consensus_support_tree.contree
~/reducta_clean/katia_outgroup/07_tree/FINAL_reducta_camilla_katia_IQTREE_report.iqtree

Interpretation:
The tree places Neptis rivularis and Neptis sappho as the most distant outgroups. The non-reducta Limenitis outgroups are closer to L. reducta. Camilla forms a clade, and populi/helmanni are placed outside or near the Camilla/reducta relationship depending on branch placement.
